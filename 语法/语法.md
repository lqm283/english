# 句子成分

## 主语

    定义： 主语是一个句子叙述的主体，也就是一句话主要叙述的对象或者主要将的内容。
示例：

1. <font color="red">I</font> am a teacher.   "I" 是主语
2. <font color="red">Chinese</font> is hard.    "Chinese" 是主语

### 能够做主语的词语

能够充当主语的词语：名词、代词、数词、动词不定式、动名词、句子、其他...

#### 名词做主语

1. <font color="red">Nick</font> is a teacher.
2. <font color="red">Jack</font> is a teacher.
3. <font color="red">Lily</font> is a student.
4. <font color="red">Guang Xi</font> is beautiful.
5. <font color="red">The book</font> is expensive.
6. <font color="red">English</font> is easy.
7. <font color="red">China is</font> very large.

#### 代词做主语

1. <font color="red">I</font> am a teacher.
2. <font color="red">She</font> is beautiful.
3. <font color="red">You</font> are rich.
4. <font color="red">He</font> is very handsome.
5. <font color="red">It</font> is cheap.
6. <font color="red">We</font> are busy.
7. <font color="red">They</font> are happy.
8. <font color="red">This</font> is my pen.
9. <font color="red">That</font> is her computer.

#### 数词做主语

1. The <font color="red">first</font> is important.
2. <font color="red">Eight</font> is a lucky number.

#### 动词不定式做主语

1. <font color="red">To learn English</font> is easy.
2. <font color="red">To eat an apple every day</font> is good for health.
3. <font color="red">To be a teacher</font> is hard.

#### 动名词和动名词短语做主语

1. <font color="red">Learning English</font> is funny.
2. <font color="red">Helping poor people</font> is our duty.
3. <font color="red">Being a teacher</font> is hard.

#### 句子做主语

一个句子也可以做主语，做主语的句子我们叫做主语从句。

#### 其它词语做主语

在英语里面，有些词语看起来不是名词，但是具有名词的含义。比如"old"本来是形容词，但是加上"the"之后，就变成名词的含义了，"the old"表示“老人的意思”，所以可以做主语。

## 谓语

    定义：说明主语动作或者状态的词语。或者说是说明主语做什么或者主语是什么，主语怎么样的词语。
    分类：谓语分为动作型谓语和状态型谓语。

### 动作型谓语

动作型谓语由动词构成，动作型谓语又分为简单谓语和复合谓语。

#### 简单谓语

一个实义动词或者一个动词短语都可以构成简单的谓语。

1. I <font color="green">work</font> very day.
2. I <font color="green">run</font> every day.
3. I <font color="green">love</font> you.
4. I <font color="green">speak</font> Chinese.
5. She <font color="green">looks after</font> her brother.
6. I <font color="green">get up</font> at six.

#### 复合谓语

复合谓语就是由简单谓语再加上其他词语，共同做谓语的情况。主要有以下三种形式:

+ **情态动词+实义动词或者动词短语的原型.**
  1. I <font color="green">can speak</font> Cantonese.
  2. I <font color="green">can look</font> after her.
+ **助动词+实义动词或者动词短语的原型。**
  1. I <font color="green">will buy</font> a  new book tomorrow.
  2. I <font color="green">will get up</font> early tomorrow.
+ **助动词+其他的动词形式(be + doing 或 have/has + done)**
  1. I <font color="green">am working</font> now.
  2. I <font color="green">have learned</font> English for 10 years.

### 状态型谓语

状态型的谓语由"系动词+表语"构成。
系动词主要由"be"充当。

1. I <font color="green">am happy</font>.
2. He <font color="green">is a doctor</font>.
3. You <font color="green">are cool</font>.
4. They <font color="green">are students</font>.
5. I <font color="green">am an English teacher</font>.

## 宾语

    定义： 宾语就是动作的对象或者承受者

### 能够做宾语的词语

能够充当主语的词语：名词、代词、数词、动词不定式、动名词、句子、其他...

#### 名词做宾语

1. I buy a <font color="greed">book</font> every year.
2. I buy <font color="greed">apples</font> every day.
3. You believe <font color="greed">Nick</font>.
4. She likes <font color="greed">Nick</font>.
5. She likes <font color="greed">music</font>.
6. I bought a <font color="greed">book</font> yesterday.
7. I borrowed a <font color="greed">pen</font> from him.

#### 代词做宾语

I believe <font color="greed">her</font>.
You believe <font color="greed">me</font>.
You believe <font color="greed">them</font>.
You believe <font color="greed">us</font>.
She loves <font color="greed">him</font>.
I love <font color="greed">her</font>.
I told <font color="greed">them</font> a story.

#### 数词做宾语：

I want <font color="greed">two</font>.

## 表语

    定义： 表语的定义与状态型谓语一致，说明主语是什么或者怎么样的词语就是表语。表语其实属于谓语的范畴，但是不能说表语就是谓语，它只是谓语的一部分，"系动词+表语"才是谓语。
    系动词和表语是不能分开的，没有系动词就没有表语，没有表语就没有系动词。

### 能做表语的词语

名词、形容词、介词短语、名词所有格、动词不定式、动名词、代词、数词、分词、副词、句子…

#### 名词做表语

I am <font color="orange">Nick</font>.
You are my <font color="orange">friend</font>.
She is a <font color="orange">nurse</font>.
He is a <font color="orange">doctor</font>.

#### 形容词做表语

I am <font color="orange">rich</font>.
You are <font color="orange">busy</font>.
We are <font color="orange">happy</font>.
They are <font color="orange">lucky</font>.
It is <font color="orange">beautiful</font>.
The house is <font color="orange">big</font>.

#### 介词短语做表语

I am <font color="orange">in Guangxi</font>.
She is <font color="orange">in Guangxi</font>.
You are <font color="orange">in Beijing</font>.
They are <font color="orange">in Shanghai</font>.
We are <font color="orange">in Ghuangzhou</font>.
She is <font color="orange">in the classroom</font>.
She is <font color="orange">under the tree</font>.
They are <font color="orange">on the playground</font>.
